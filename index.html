About the Camp

Two sports.
One perfect week.

Adriatic Sports Getaways combines daily padel coaching with morning pilates sessions, set against the clearest water in the Mediterranean. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned player, our certified coaches tailor every session to your level.

Camp Straško in Novalja is one of the Adriatic's most beloved coastal retreats — pine trees, white stone, and sea just steps from your accommodation.
